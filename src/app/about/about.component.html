What We Do

Time is the most important entity for a productive person. Reading a book takes ample time. We at Knowing Books provide you the best short description of an entire book and its author so that you can decide whether a book is worth your time.

About the founder

Tanmay Thakur is an avid reader of books and knows that books add values to a person's career and life. He founded Knowing books as a personal project and soon elaborated it to provide value on a larger scale.

Knowing Books does not promote books/authors out of personal interests. The Featured Authors/Books are purely based on the analytics and sales. We do not intend to harm/degrade any author/individual/publication.
